CLOCK example

 

 

Example 1

 

Definition of the clock = 1 MHz with resolution of 10ns (1% of 1Mhz)

DECLARE CHANNEL S1=1;

DECLARE CHANNEL S2=4;

DECLARE VARIABLE VAR1;

CLOCK 1MHZ;

TIMING TIM1;

PERIOD 10C;

STROBE 3C;

ENDTIMING;

START PRG1;

IH S1 S2;

.

.

ENDTEST;
