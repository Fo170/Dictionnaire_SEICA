### Syntax

PERIOD time; 

time

Represents the duration of the executive phase of the pattern.

__________________________

### Note

The PERIOD time must always be specified 
 in the timing declaration.

if NCLOCK is specified the duration of the period will be a multiple 
 of the NCLOCK
